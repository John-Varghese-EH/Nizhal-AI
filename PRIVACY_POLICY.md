# Privacy Policy

**Nizhal AI - Personal AI Companion**  
*Last Updated: January 25, 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nizhal AI ("the Application", "we", "us") handles your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

**Key Principle**: Nizhal AI is designed as a privacy-first application. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to use cloud features.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Profile Information**: Name, preferences, personality settings
- **Conversations**: Chat history with the AI assistant
- **Voice Data**: Voice recordings when using voice commands (processed locally)
- **Calendar Events**: If you connect Google Calendar
- **Tasks & Notes**: Your tasks, reminders, and notes
- **Mood Data**: Emotion logs you create in the Mood Tracker

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Camera Data**: Facial expressions for emotion detection (processed locally, never stored permanently)
- **System Information**: Device type, OS version, app version (for troubleshooting)
- **Usage Analytics**: Feature usage patterns (optional, local only)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **NOT** send your conversations to our servers
- We do **NOT** sell or share your personal data with third parties
- We do **NOT** track your location (except for weather, with your permission)
- We do **NOT** collect biometric data beyond temporary emotion detection

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Location |
|-----------|---------|------------------|
| Conversations | AI responses, memory | Local device |
| Emotions | Avatar reactions, mood tracking | Local device |
| Preferences | Personalization | Local device |
| Calendar | Schedule management | Google (if connected) |
| Voice | Voice commands | Processed locally, not stored |

## 4. Third-Party Services

When you use certain features, data may be shared with third-party services:

### 4.1 AI Providers
If you configure external AI providers:
- **OpenAI**: Conversations sent to OpenAI API
- **Google AI**: Conversations sent to Google Gemini API
- **Anthropic**: Conversations sent to Claude API
- **Local LLMs**: No external transmission

**Your Choice**: You can use local LLMs (Ollama) for complete privacy.

### 4.2 Other Services
- **OpenWeatherMap**: City name or coordinates for weather (no personal data)
- **LiveKit**: Voice/video data during calls (encrypted, not stored)
- **Google Calendar API**: Calendar data (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)

## 5. Data Storage & Security

### 5.1 Local Storage
- Data is stored in encrypted local storage
- Located in your user data directory
- Accessible only by the Application

### 5.2 Security Measures
- No cloud database (unless you enable sync)
- API keys stored locally and encrypted
- Network requests use HTTPS

### 5.3 Data Retention
- Data persists until you delete it
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- No backups are made without your action

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

### 6.1 Access Your Data
- View all stored data in the Application settings
- Export your data as JSON

### 6.2 Delete Your Data
- Delete individual items (memories, conversations, tasks)
- Clear all data through settings
- Uninstall to remove everything

### 6.3 Control Your Data
- Choose which features to enable/disable
- Revoke camera/microphone permissions
- Disconnect third-party services

## 7. Camera & Microphone

### 7.1 Camera Access
- Used for emotion detection (optional)
- Processed locally using TensorFlow.js
- Video data is **never** recorded or transmitted
- Can be disabled anytime in settings

### 7.2 Microphone Access
- Used for voice commands and LiveKit calls
- Voice recognition processed locally or via Whisper API
- Can be disabled anytime

## 8. Children's Privacy

Nizhal AI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 9. International Users

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you use cloud AI providers, data may be processed in their respective jurisdictions (USA for OpenAI/Anthropic, varies for Google).
