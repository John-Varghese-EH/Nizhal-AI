# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: January 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Nizhal AI ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our desktop companion application ("the Software").

## 2. Privacy Mode

Nizhal AI includes a **Privacy Mode** toggle that allows you to control data processing:

### When Privacy Mode is ON (Local Only):
- ✅ All AI processing uses local Ollama models
- ✅ No data is sent to cloud services
- ✅ Camera is disabled
- ✅ No voice data leaves your device
- ✅ Maximum privacy protection

### When Privacy Mode is OFF (Cloud AI):
- AI requests may be sent to third-party providers (Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic)
- Third-party terms and privacy policies apply
- Camera frames may be processed by cloud AI for vision features

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Local Data (Stored on Your Device)
- **Preferences**: Settings, persona selections, API keys
- **Conversation History**: Chat logs (stored locally)
- **Memory Entries**: Journal and notes you create
- **Character Settings**: Display preferences, positions

### 3.2 Data Sent to Third Parties (When Privacy Mode is OFF)
- **AI Providers (Gemini/OpenAI/Anthropic)**:
  - Text messages for generating responses
  - Camera frames (if vision enabled)
  - Voice transcriptions
- **ElevenLabs** (if configured):
  - Text for voice synthesis

## 4. How We Use Information

We use your information to:
- Provide AI companion functionality
- Remember your preferences
- Improve the user experience
- Enable voice and vision features

## 5. Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All user data is stored locally on your device
- **No Servers**: Nizhal AI does not operate servers that store user data
- **API Keys**: Stored locally in encrypted format
- **Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all local data

## 7. Camera and Microphone

### Camera Access
- Used for AI vision features in full dashboard mode
- Disabled automatically when Privacy Mode is ON
- Frames are processed in real-time, not stored
- You can toggle camera on/off at any time

### Microphone Access
- Used for voice commands and Gemini Live
- Audio is streamed in real-time, not stored
- Push-to-talk or continuous modes available

## 8. Children's Privacy

Nizhal AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Local-only storage of sensitive data
- Encrypted API key storage
- Privacy Mode for complete local processing
- No unnecessary data collection

## 10. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Enable Privacy Mode at any time
- Delete local data by uninstalling
- Review what data is stored locally
- Disable camera and microphone access

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be noted by the "Last Updated" date.
